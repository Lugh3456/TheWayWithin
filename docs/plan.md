# Project Plan — The Way Within

## Project Name

The Way Within — Classical Chinese Wisdom Portal (修身之道)

---

# Vision

Create a master portal that unifies a growing collection of classical Chinese wisdom mini sites under a single, beautifully designed hub. Each mini site is an independent repository covering a distinct classical text or teacher. The portal acts as the entry point, presenting each site as a card and linking out to it.

The collection focuses on texts and teachers relevant to self-cultivation, moral philosophy, and classical statecraft.

---

# Target Audience

- Students of Chinese philosophy, history, and classical literature
- Readers exploring Daoist, Confucian, and traditional Chinese thought
- Professionals interested in classical wisdom and self-discipline
- General readers curious about Chinese intellectual heritage

---

# Core Features

- Single-page portal with card grid of mini sites
- Card for each mini site: symbol, Chinese name, English name, description, tags, link
- Coming Soon placeholder card for next addition
- About section explaining the collection
- Skip link for keyboard accessibility
- Unified visual design shared with all mini sites

---

# Design Principles

- Calm, scholarly, spacious, welcoming
- Warm parchment background with deep ink header
- Gold accents for interactive elements
- Both Chinese and English naming on all cards
- Tags to indicate the type of content (Philosophy, Divination, Self-Cultivation, etc.)

---

# Non-Goals

- Not a content hosting site — articles live on the mini sites, not here
- Not a search engine for classical content
- No user accounts or personalisation
- No server-side logic
